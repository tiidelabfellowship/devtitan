The Intelligent Investor


"The Intelligent Investor" by Benjamin Graham. This groundbreaking book has been an indispensable guide for investors and a cornerstone of value investing since its publication in 1949. Join us as we delve into the world of financial wisdom and learn from the principles outlined by Graham, a renowned economist and investor.


"The Intelligent Investor" provides invaluable insights into the art of investing, emphasizing the importance of a disciplined and rational approach. Graham's timeless wisdom teaches us to analyze stocks, assess their intrinsic value, and make informed investment decisions. Throughout the book, he emphasizes the significance of long-term thinking, risk management.


In this book club, we will engage in thought-provoking discussions, share personal experiences, and analyze real-world investment scenarios.

About the Author

Benjamin Graham


The esteemed author of "The Intelligent Investor," was a pioneer in the field of value investing and is widely regarded as one of the greatest investment advisors of the 20th century. Born in London in 1894, Graham later moved to the United States and became a highly influential economist, professor, and financial expert.
